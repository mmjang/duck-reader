Tell us: have you been affected by the shortage of school uniform in the UK?

Have you been able to buy new school uniform for your child this year? Photograph: Ian West/PA
Guardian community team

Last modified on Wed 25 Aug 2021 11.06 BST

Businesses and retailers in the UK have been experiencing a supply chain crisis with stock levels at their lowest since 1983.

With the new school year starting in a few weeks, uniform and shoes for children have become difficult to come by and we would like to hear from parents about how they have been affected by the shortage.

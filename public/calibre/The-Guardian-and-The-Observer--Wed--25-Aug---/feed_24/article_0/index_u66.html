Tokyo 2020 Paralympic Games: full medal table

Mon 23 Aug 2021 12.00 BST

About the medal table

As is traditional, the table prioritises the number of gold medals won. On this basis in 2016, China led the Paralympics field with 107 golds, with Great Britain second on 64 and Ukraine third with 41. If countries have the same number of gold medals, the order is then dictated by which has the most silvers, and finally bronze if the numbers are still identical.

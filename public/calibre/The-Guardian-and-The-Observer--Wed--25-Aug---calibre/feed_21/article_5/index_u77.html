Harry Kane and the endless Tottenham transfer saga – Football Weekly Extra

Max Rushden is joined by Barry Glendenning, Jonathan Liew and Simon Burnton to discuss the Spurs striker’s predicament and preview the weekend’s action

How to listen to podcasts: everything you need to know

Presented by Max Rushden with Barry Glendenning, Jonathan Liew and Simon Burnton. Produced by Joel Grove.

Thu 19 Aug 2021 15.00 BST

Rate, review, share on Apple Podcasts, Soundcloud, Audioboom, Mixcloud, Acast and Stitcher, and join the conversation on Facebook, Twitter and email.

On the podcast today: we discuss Harry Kane’s ongoing transfer situation, with training pictures and tabloid whispers doing little to change the Spurs forward’s situation.

We also look forward to another round of Premier League fixtures as Arsenal take on Chelsea and Nuno goes back to Molineux with Spurs. Elsewhere, as fans boo players taking the knee at some EFL fixtures, we ask whether clubs, managers and players have a responsibility to speak out in support.

Plus, the panel try to understand Uefa’s proposed €7bn bailout fund, Aaron Ramsdale heads for the Emirates and some questionable new third kit designs.
